With a fleet of over 5,000 ambulances and a presence in more than 550 cities, RED Health is dedicated to providing quick and reliable medical assistance, from ambulance services to telemedicine and beyond. We are here to support you and your loved ones through every step of your healthcare journey.

Emergency Rooms

Emergency Department defines an emergency department as a hospital facility that is staffed 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, and provides unscheduled outpatient services to patients whose condition requires immediate attention.

Free Check-Ups

Regular health check-ups are a must for every individual. Both, young and elderly should undergo health checkups to keep a complete check on their health. If you possess a health insurance policy, do not hesitate to make the most of the free health checkup feature that your health insurance company may have included in the policy. If you are new to health insurance, learn that your health insurance policy may comprise a free health checkup feature, which would be of great help in assessing your health without spending a significant amount.
